Smart Home

Power revolutionises home energy management, integrating seamlessly with your home. It monitors energy usage in real-time, offers Al-driven analytics, and evolves through machine learning..

EMBRACE ENERGY INTELLIGENCE

Power transcends the traditional smart home device category; it's an all-encompassing energy management system crafted to integrate flawlessly into any home setup. With Power, residents can now monitor and control their energy usage with unparalleled accuracy.

Seamless Integration

Power is engineered to operate in harmony with leading smart home platforms such as Google Home, Amazon Alexa, Hive, Nest and Apple HomeKit, facilitating effortless device and energy consumption management via voice commands or a mobile app.

Make Informed Decisions

With Power, residents gain tools to make informed energy decisions, saving on bills and reducing their carbon footprint. Power's analytics show device usage details, helping identify inefficient appliances and schedule tasks during off-peak hours.

Environment Monitoring

In addition to managing your electricity and gas usage like traditional systems; Power will help you organise and manage your devices, inform you about your domestic water usage and even about the quality of the air in your home.

Machine Learning

As time progresses, Power's machine learning algorithms enhance its energy monitoring and optimisation capabilities. It will learn from your usage patterns and preferences, to deliver increasingly precise and tailored energy-saving recommendations.

Sustainability

Power's ethos extends beyond financial savings; it's about diminishing our carbon footprint. By optimising both energy and water consumption, Power will aid in creating a smarter consumer - fostering a more sustainable future.

Our Pledge

Power is dedicated to propelling progress towards a more sustainable world. By enhancing energy usage efficiency, Power benefits not only individual residents but also contributes to the collective goal of energy efficiency and environmental stewardship.
